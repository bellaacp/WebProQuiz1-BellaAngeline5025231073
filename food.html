Local Food

Rawon

Rawon is a black beef soup made with keluak (black nuts), giving it a rich and flavorful taste. It’s one of Surabaya’s most iconic dishes!

Rujak Cingur

Rujak Cingur is a traditional Javanese dish made from boiled vegetables and fruits, with a signature peanut and shrimp paste sauce.

Lontong Balap

Lontong Balap is a delicious combination of rice cakes, bean sprouts, fried tofu, and savory lentho (fried lentil cakes) with a soy sauce broth.
