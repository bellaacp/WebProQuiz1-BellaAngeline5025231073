Tourist Places

Monumen Kapal Selam

This submarine monument is a must-visit for history buffs, showcasing Indonesia’s naval history. Visitors can explore the interior of an actual submarine!

Heroes Monument (Tugu Pahlawan)

An iconic monument commemorating the Battle of Surabaya, a major event in Indonesia’s fight for independence.

Sanggar Agung Temple

This temple is home to a striking 36-meter four-faced Buddha statue, symbolizing peace and devotion. Beyond its stunning beauty, it provides a profound spiritual experience, making it a must-visit for those seeking cultural and religious insight into Surabaya’s heritage.

Suramadu Bridge

The remarkable bridge connecting Surabaya and Madura. Stretching 5.4 km, this national landmark offers breathtaking views and is perfect for a leisurely stroll or bike ride.

Tunjungan Plaza

Experience the vibrant heart of Surabaya at Tunjungan Plaza, one of the city’s top shopping destinations! As one of the first modern malls in Surabaya, it boasts over 500 retail stores offering everything from trendy boutiques and bookstores to supermarkets and a variety of dining options.

Old Town Surabaya

Step into the charm of Old Town Surabaya, where vibrant historical buildings date back to the 16th century. This picturesque area is perfect for photography with delightful shops filled with unique souvenirs. Immerse yourself in the rich heritage!
